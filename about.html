About MeAbout Me

Oleksandr Kuskov is a passionate and experienced Icarian Games performer. With over 13 years of experience, he has toured internationally, captivating audiences with his strength, balance, and artistic expression. Er sucht ständig nach neuen Herausforderungen, Kooperationen und innovativen Formen der Bewegung und Erzählkunst im Zirkus.
